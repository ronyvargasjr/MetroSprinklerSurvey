About

Hi, I’m Rony, founder of Metro Sprinkler Survey — a New York–based freelance service specializing in fire sprinkler field surveys and documentation.

After a decade of hands-on experience working with design engineers and contractors, I saw a gap in how building systems were being documented.

Too often, outdated drawings or incomplete surveys slowed down projects and added unnecessary costs. I started Metro Sprinkler Survey to solve that problem — providing accurate, clear, and affordable field surveys that help engineers and building owners move forward with confidence.

My goal is simple: make the survey process smooth, precise, and professional, so you can focus on design and compliance knowing the field data is right.

At Metro Sprinkler Survey, every project is handled with care, communication, and a dash of New York grit.
